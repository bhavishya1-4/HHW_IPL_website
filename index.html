Welcome to The Indian Premier League 2025


The Indian Premier League (IPL) 2025 marks the 18th edition of the world's most popular and competitive T20 cricket tournament. Organized by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI), the IPL has become a global sporting spectacle, drawing top talent from across the world and attracting millions of passionate fans.

The 2025 season continues the legacy of thrilling matches, big-hitting performances, nail-biting finishes, and high entertainment. All 10 franchises from the previous seasons — including Chennai Super Kings, Delhi Capitals, Gujarat Titans, Kolkata Knight Riders, Lucknow Super Giants, Mumbai Indians, Punjab Kings, Rajasthan Royals, Royal Challengers Bengaluru, and Sunrisers Hyderabad — returned with renewed strategies, exciting new players, and a hunger for glory.

The tournament is set to feature a total of 74 matches, with each team playing 14 league matches before the playoffs. The IPL 2025 promises to deliver a blend of cricketing excellence, fierce rivalries, and unforgettable moments, as teams vie for the coveted IPL trophy and the title of champions.

Fans can expect a month-long cricketing extravaganza, with matches held in iconic stadiums across India. The IPL 2025 is not just a tournament; it's a celebration of cricket, culture, and the spirit of competition that unites fans from all walks of life.

TEAMS

Here are the teams participating in IPL 2025:

  • Chennai Super Kings

    The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are one of the most successful franchises in IPL history, known for their consistent performances and strong leadership under MS Dhoni. With a loyal fan base, CSK has won multiple IPL titles and continues to be a formidable force in the tournament.

  • Delhi Capitals

    The Delhi Capitals (DC) have emerged as a competitive team in recent seasons, showcasing a blend of young talent and experienced players. With a focus on building a strong squad, DC aims to secure their first IPL title in 2025.

  • Gujarat Titans

    The Gujarat Titans (GT) made their debut in IPL 2022 and quickly established themselves as a strong contender. With a balanced team and strategic gameplay, GT is looking to build on their early success and challenge for the title in 2025.

  • Kolkata Knight Riders

    The Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) are one of the most popular franchises, known for their passionate fan base and iconic purple and gold colors. With a history of success, KKR aims to reclaim their position at the top of the IPL standings in 2025.

  • Lucknow Super Giants

    The Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) are a relatively new team in the IPL, having joined the league in 2022. With a focus on building a strong squad and developing young talent, LSG is determined to make their mark in the tournament.

  • Mumbai Indians

    The Mumbai Indians (MI) are the most successful franchise in IPL history, boasting multiple championship titles. Known for their star-studded lineup and strong team culture, MI is always a favorite to win the tournament.

  • Punjab Kings

    The Punjab Kings (PBKS) have been a competitive team in the IPL, known for their explosive batting and dynamic gameplay. With a focus on building a balanced squad, PBKS aims to secure their first IPL title in 2025.

  • Rajasthan Royals

    The Rajasthan Royals (RR) are known for their innovative approach and emphasis on nurturing young talent. With a rich history in the IPL, RR is looking to reclaim their former glory and challenge for the title in 2025.

  • Royal Challengers Bengaluru

    The Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) are known for their explosive batting lineup and passionate fan base. Despite not having won the IPL title yet, RCB remains a formidable team with a strong desire to claim their first championship in 2025.

  • Sunrisers Hyderabad

    The Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) are known for their strong bowling attack and disciplined gameplay. With a history of success in the IPL, SRH is determined to make a strong comeback and compete for the title in 2025.

NEWS

Stay upto date with the latest headlines of this seasons IPL:

RCB Clinches Victory in Thrilling Final



In a nail-biting finish, Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) emerged victorious against Punjab Kings (PBKS) in the IPL 2025 final. RCB's star player, Virat Kohli, played a crucial role with a stunning century, leading his team to their first IPL title.

RCB Storms Into IPL 2025 Playoffs After Dramatic Turnaround!



After a shaky start to the tournament, RCB bounced back with six consecutive wins, thanks to spectacular performances by Virat Kohli, Faf du Plessis, and Mohammed Siraj. Their final league match against Rajasthan Royals was a do-or-die encounter, and RCB rose to the occasion with a dominant all-round performance.
RCB now joins the playoff lineup alongside PBKS(Punjab Kings). Fans across the country are celebrating, hoping that this might finally be the year when RCB brings home the trophy for the first time.
Virat Kohli, after the match, said:
"We believed in our process, stuck to our strengths, and the results followed. The job’s not done yet—we're hungry for the title!"

CSK have their worst season.



In a shocking and disappointing turn of events, Chennai Super Kings (CSK)—one of the most successful franchises in IPL history—have become the first team to be eliminated from the IPL 2025 season.
Plagued by inconsistency, injuries, and underwhelming performances from key players, CSK managed only 3 wins in 10 matches, failing to find their rhythm throughout the tournament. The absence of veteran form, a struggling middle order, and a weak bowling attack proved costly.
This marks CSK’s worst performance in IPL history, raising questions about the future of the team’s aging core and leadership transition following MS Dhoni’s retirement at the end of IPL 2024.
Head Coach Stephen Fleming stated:
"It’s a tough pill to swallow. We didn’t click as a unit this year. It’s time to rebuild and come back stronger."
